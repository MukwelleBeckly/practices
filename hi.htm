7-Day European Adventure

Main Cities Visited

  • Paris
    1. Visit the Eiffel Tower and Seine River cruise
    2. Explore the Louvre and walk along Champs-Élysées
  • Rome
    1. Tour the Colosseum and Roman Forum
    2. Visit Vatican City and St. Peter’s Basilica
  • Barcelona
    1. Stroll through Park Güell and admire Gaudí’s architecture
    2. Relax at Barceloneta Beach and enjoy local tapas

Key Travel Terms

Itinerary
A planned schedule of travel events and destinations.
  • Daily sightseeing schedule
  • Arrival and departure times
  • Transportation details
Accommodation
Places where travelers stay during a trip.
  • Hotel
  • Hostel
  • Vacation rental
  • Bed & Breakfast
Excursion
A short journey or trip, especially one engaged in as a leisure activity.
  • Wine tasting tour in Tuscany
  • Guided hike in the Swiss Alps
  • Museum visit in Amsterdam
  • Cooking class in Madrid
