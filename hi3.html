Fitness and Nutrition Essentials

Core Components of a Balanced Fitness Routine

  • Cardiovascular Training
    1. Running or jogging
    2. Cycling
    3. Swimming
  • Strength Training
    1. Weight lifting
    2. Bodyweight exercises (push-ups, squats)
    3. Resistance band workouts
  • Flexibility and Mobility
    1. Static stretching
    2. Dynamic stretching
    3. Yoga or Pilates
  • Rest and Recovery
    1. Active recovery (walking, gentle yoga)
    2. Scheduled rest days
    3. Sleep and mindfulness

Key Nutrition Terms

Macronutrients
Nutrients required in large amounts that provide energy and support body function.
  • Proteins: chicken, tofu, lentils
  • Carbohydrates: brown rice, sweet potatoes, whole grains
  • Fats: avocados, nuts, olive oil
Hydration
Maintaining optimal fluid balance in the body for performance and health.
  • Water
  • Electrolyte drinks
  • Hydrating foods like cucumber and watermelon
Recovery
Processes that help the body rebuild and recharge after physical activity.
